Scale prototyping and production with affordable, industrial-quality SLA and SLS 3D printers.
Formlabs is a desktop 3D printing company that also worked with US hospitals to 3D print nasopharyngeal (NP) swabs needed to collect samples for COVID-19 testing. A Formlabs printer could produce approximately 300 print swabs per build with the same material as Surgical Guide Resin. The company received the FDA’s Class I exemption and its swabs were used for patients at University of San Francisco and Northwell Health.
