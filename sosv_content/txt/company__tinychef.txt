NutriChef is an AI-powered platform designed to assist dietitians in delivering personalized nutrition management. It streamlines client onboarding, analyzes dietary profiles, generates tailored meal plans, and interprets medical data, including blood reports. The platform also features a client-side application, TinyChef.ai, enabling clients to complete intake forms, upload medical records, and photograph meals for automatic analysis. Since its inception in 2017, NutriChef has raised $3 million and is currently focusing on user growth and revenue expansion, aiming to achieve $100,000 in monthly recurring revenue by March 2025.
